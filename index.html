I'm Beatriz Novaes, a graphic designer based in Maceió, Brazil, with a writer's eye. I work with visual identity, social media, e-commerce design and Amazon A+ content — always searching for the right phrase the image hasn't said yet.

Trabalho com marcas que querem soar autênticas: do rebranding completo ao design de produto. E, fora do expediente, escrevo poesia — o que me ensinou que todo bom design conta uma história. I work with brands that want to feel authentic: from full rebrands to product design. And after hours, I write poetry — which taught me that every good design tells a story.
